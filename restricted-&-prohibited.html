Restricted & Prohibited Items

Categories and specific products that you need to pay special attention to. Some products are completely forbidden from exporting out of the US, some require additional steps to ensure the shipment can be processed, and some can be exported but you must ensure the destination country will allow the import of the same.

Prohibited Items

Items listed as Prohibited cannot be shipped via our service under any circumstances. We have provided categories, brief summaries, and example products for things that are Prohibited. However, please know these examples are not exhaustive and these categories of Prohibited products are more expansive that what is listed.
