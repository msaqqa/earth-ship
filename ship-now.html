How EARTH SHIP Gets Great Rates

Long-Term Partnerships with Top Carriers

When you ship from the US with PackageHopper, you can be sure you’re getting fast, reliable international delivery at great rates. Thanks to our long-term partnerships, we can pass along some of the best rates around, that wouldn’t usually be available to the public.

Simplified Processing

With PackageHopper, you share all of your shipment information, pack your items, and pay online—all from the comfort of your own home. Then, just drop off your shipment at a local location whenever it is convenient for you! By conveniently prepping your items at home, and taking them to the local drop-off, we can offer cheaper prices by cutting down on administrative costs.
